Welcome! / ¡Bienvenido!

Hello! I'm David, an electrical engineer and proud Colombian-American, among many other things. I have a strong background in both hardware design and programming, and love collaborating with and learning from people of all walks of life.

I graduated with my B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering in May of 2024, and hope to continue my education while gaining industry knowledge and improving my skills in a professional setting.

My current and foremost areas of interest include, but are not limited to: Silicon Microfrabrication and chip design, IoT projects, signal processing for sound design and music analysis, and understanding more of the math behind neural networks.

When I'm not working or studying, I enjoy practicing sports, writing and producing music, and spending time with my family.

Thank you for your time and for visiting my wesbite, I hope you enjoy exploring my page!
